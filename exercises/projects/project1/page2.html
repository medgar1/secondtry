Here are some other really chill artists who are changing the music scene!

Mitski

Mitski is a singer-songwriter who has a talent for combining sad meaningful lyrics with full compositions. Her album "Bury me at Makeout Creek" has been praised by various sources, such as Rolling Stones.

Hinds

Hinds is a band situated in Madrid based on free-flowing garage rock. They just started last year, but are already selling out concert venues in the US.
